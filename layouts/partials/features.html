Folders that make themselves🛡️

Nobody wants to go through the hassle of organizing all the papers they read. Thankfully, now you don't have to because we do it for you.

Smart-search integrated file system🚀

With semantic search integrated in your file system, you'll never need to fret if you only remember the idea of a paper.

Informed chats for your research 📈

When you have hard questions, don't worry about parsing through all your research. Just ask your personal chat, and it will pull up the answers to your questions with the paper they're in.

Summaries for all your documents 🦉

Just like your real brain, Second Brain summarizes all documents you pass to it so you can pull up the key points faster.

Upload all your files or even web pages ✨

Second Brain comes with its very own chrome extension so you can scrape articles you're reading on the web. That way, you don't need to worry about downloading everything you're interested in.

Organize your data in countless ways 🧰

With the option to select multiple views or 'spaces', we can keep your documents organized however makes the most sense to you at any time, and you can even swap between all the views you have.
